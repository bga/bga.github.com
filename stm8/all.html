Alalysis

  • STM8S001J3 - generic mcu. VCap pin reduces usable pins down to 5. Or 4 if you leave SWD. No MISO.
  • STM8L001J3 - same as STM8S001J. Same cost. But huge EEPROM. No ADC but there are analog comparators. No stupid Vcap pin. Much better.
  • STM8L050J3 - combines pros of prev ones for 150% price. 12 bit ADC instead 10 bit. Also DMA.

Analysis

  • STM8S003F3 - most basic and cheapest one. Only 128 bytes EEPROM. 100 flash rewrites. For final mass production.
  • STM8S103F3 - Same as STM8S003F3 but more EEPROM. And 10k flash rewrites. For debuging.
  • STM8L101F2/STM8L101F3 - Just better than STM8S103F3 for same price. ADC is replaced to analog comparators. Not pin compatible to STM8S003F3/STM8S103F3
  • STM8L051F3 - Same as STM8L051J3 but more pins. 12 bit ADC, DMA. Awesome.
  • STM8L151F3 - Full flesh ... for 8kb flash. 12 bit ADC, RTC, analog comparators, DMA, internal Vref ADC & temp sensor, encoder api.

So we have 2 classes. 2 STM8S ready for drop in replacement. And 4 pin compatible STM8L.
STM8L - SWIM at pin 3, STM8S - SWIM at pin 18. Rest VCC, GND, NRST at same pins.
